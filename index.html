About Us

Unique Solutions For Innovation & Enterpreneurship!

The Best Agency to Improve Your Business

Established in 2017, Innovation SL Ltd (Innosl) is an Entrepreneurship Support Organisation (ESO) and an Innovation & Technology Management company. We own the Freetown Pitch Night, Innovations Axis Ltd (an incubator/accelerator and co-working space) and are the Global Entrepreneurship Network affiliate in Sierra Leone. Innosl was established for the purpose of impacting Sierra Leone’s entrepreneurship and Innovation ecosystem, by identifying and implementing effective programs, initiatives, projects and policies that foster and support Sierra Leone’s entrepreneurs and Innovation ecosystem.

01

Ecosystem

rom our establishment in 2017 our focus has been on developing value creating and high growth entrepreneurs as opposed to rent seeking entrepreneurs. Further, we have sought to deviate from the standard approach to nurturing innovators- through innovation competitions that focus on the top 3 winners out of 50 submissions. These top three will get cash prizes with little and sometimes no follow up. We believe that for Entrepreneurship and Innovation to play the crucial role in creating jobs and growing the Sierra Leonean economy, we need to develop value creating and high growth entrepreneurs. For this to happen, the Entrepreneurial Ecosystem must be in place and enabled. This is why our approach has been based on developing components of the ecosystem; and enabling other parts of the ecosystem that is suited to nurturing value creating and high growth potential startups and scale ups.

02

Incubator and Accelerator

In 2018 we began our incubation and acceleration program. The program is located at Kingtom which is a space we have under a separate company- Innovations Axis. At the end of 2019, we have 12 start ups under incubation in various stages of the development. Already one of our start- ups under incubation, has generated revenues of 90 million leones. In 2020, this same start-up will generate a further 350million leones. Five of our start ups are at the validation start phase and this process will continue for another six months in 2020. Validation remains one of the most part of our incubation program, where the focus is on an MVP that is going to address a tier 1 problem for a particular customer segment.

Track Record

Since 2001 InnoSL’s founder and MD has been developing start-ups and accelerating scale ups internationally. In Sierra Leone, since InnoSL’s founding in 2017, we have worked with, and supported, the growth of 25+ start-ups and SMEs and assisted other ecosystem players in doing the same. We started the first full service combined incubator and accelerator in Sierra Leone in 2018 which, since inception, has incubated and accelerated 16 start-ups, many of whom came through Freetown Pitch Night, a monthly pitch event we organise.
